Company Description

FastCharge.me is the developer and operator of highly-reliable DC charging solutions for EVs. Our solution includes all hardware and software, designed to leverage a combination of high reliability power electronics and AI technology to enhance user experience and optimize operations.  Our designs provides vandal-proof fast charging stations in keeping  with California’s mandates for EV sales, EV conversions along with the Advanced Clean Cars and Trucks Regulations being adopted nationwide.

FAQ

What is FastCharge.me?
FastCharge.me is a mobile app that helps you to quickly and easily find and pay for electric vehicle (EV) charging at FastCharge.me DC fast charging stations.
Is the FastCharge.me app free to use?
Yes, the FastCharge.me app is free to download and use.
What iOS versions does FastCharge.me support?
FastCharge.me is compatible with iOS 13 and later.

Account & Setup

Do I need to create an account to use the FastCharge.me app?
Yes, you need to create an account to use the app's full features, including saving your favorite charging stations and payment information.
How do I create an account?
You can create an account using your email address or by linking your Google or Apple account.
How do I add my vehicle information?
After creating an account, go to the "Profile" section and tap "Add Vehicle." You'll be able to enter your vehicle's make, model, and year. This information helps the app find compatible charging stations.

Finding & Using Charging Stations

How do I find a charging station?
On the app's home screen, you'll see a map with nearby charging stations. You can also use the search bar to find stations by address, city, or zip code.
How do I know if a station is available?
Each station on the map will show its real-time availability status (e.g., available, occupied, offline).
How do I start a charging session?
Once you're at a station, tap the "Start Charging" button in the app. You'll need to select a payment method if you haven't already saved one.
How do I pay for charging?
You can pay for charging using a saved credit or debit card or through Apple Pay.
Can I reserve a charging station in advance?
Currently, the app does not support reserving charging stations.

Troubleshooting & Support

What should I do if I encounter an issue with a charging station?
If you have a problem with a station, please report it to us through the app. You can also contact the station owner directly using the contact information provided in the app and posted on the charger.
How do I contact FastCharge.me customer support?
You can contact our customer support team by emailing support@fastcharge.me or through the "Help & Support" section in the app.

Other

Does FastCharge.me work with all EV charging networks?
FastCharge.me is a new network of fast DC chargers. We're continuously working to expand our network of charging stations. 
Can I earn rewards or points for using the FastCharge.me app?
We may offer rewards or loyalty programs in the future. Stay tuned for updates!
